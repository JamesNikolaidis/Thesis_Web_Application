Welcome to Farmers Helper Help Page



On this page you will find some informations about the problem or questions which your Company could have.
First of all , on top of the page is web site's menu. With this menu you can navigate into our site.
There is the menu button "Home",which it will send you to the main page.After this button is the "Profile" option where you can see your Company's information and if you want you can edit them. Next is the option called "Product" which breaks into two new options 1)New Product, where you can add a new product to our database and make it visible to the consumer in miliseconds and 2) View Products, where you can see in a table all the products that your company save and delete or edit them.
Further up it is the "Distribiuters" option , which give you the ability to create new Distributers which your company corporate with them, and make it easier to consumer to find you products . Before the end we find the option "Help" which is this page . Final there is the "Contact" option. With the "Contact" option you can communicate with our Company and give you feedback about any problem's that you may have.
